Importance of blood donation.

Unfortunately, there is a severe shortage for blood in our country. It is because either the eligible people are hesitant to donate blood or there isn't enough blood donation camps conducted in our society

Is blood donation harmful?

Some people assume that donating blood, means losing something permanently from your body. This is a total misconception. The human body consists of about five to six litres of blood, out of which only 350~450ml is collected from each donor This is the excess reserve of blood collected from the human body only after suitable testing.

Benefits of donating blood.

Immediately after blood donation the bone marrow spontaneously starts to produce blood cells, even before you've been served the refreshment after the blood donation. After blood donation the cell production rate increases and will bring back the levels to normal in about 2 to 3 weeks.
Blood donation is helpful to both the donor and recipient, recent studies have shown that regular blood donors are far less prone to heart attacks.
